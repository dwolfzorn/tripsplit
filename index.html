Split details

How the split is calculated

Trip costs are split evenly between all attendees after adjusting for incomes and cost of living, to ensure that all attendees pay the same amount relative to their financial situation. Income is adjusted based on cost of living index (100 = average), represented as (annual_income * (cost_of_living_index / 100) = adjusted_income).

If an expense has an exclusion tag applied, the attendees assigned to that tag are left out of that expense's split — its cost is instead divided (using the same COL-adjusted weighting) only among the remaining attendees.
